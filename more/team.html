Eng. Anthony Wandu Bsc Civil (Hons) R.Eng. ERB

Eng. Wandu is the principal and project leader for Abba & Wandu Engineers. He has 16 years experience in structural design and execution of construction projects in east Africa. He is currently in charge of contracts, design team leadership and project management in the firm. Eng.
